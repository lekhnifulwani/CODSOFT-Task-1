Welcome To Palm Paradise

About Us

We are Palm Paradise Group
An international and dynamic hotel group. Ten distinctive brands. More than 1,160 hotels in operation and under development in EMEA and APAC.

Explore the great outdoors with a variety of activities including hiking, biking, and water sports, or simply relax on our private beach. Our dedicated staff is committed to making your stay unforgettable, with personalized service and attention to every detail.

Indulge in our elegantly designed accommodations, each featuring stunning views, plush bedding, and top-notch facilities to ensure a comfortable stay. Savor gourmet cuisine at our on-site restaurants, unwind at our world-class spa, or take a refreshing dip in our infinity pool overlooking breathtaking landscapes.

Our resort offers an exquisite blend of modern amenities and natural beauty, providing the perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life.

Our Services

Wellness Facilities

An exclusive spa chain featured in all palm paradise hotels, invites you to relax and rejuvenate under the expert hands of our therapists. Indulge in a serene escape , where we offer soothing massages, invigorating body wraps and tranquil sauna sessions to enhance your well-being.

Exquisite Dining

Our award-winning restaurants provide an exciting culinary journey. From local and Caribbean fare to the Old World tastes of Southern Italy and the delicacies of Japan and Thailand, explore a world of epicurean delights right on-site.
